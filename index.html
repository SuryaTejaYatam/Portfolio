College Management System

The College Management System project is designed to streamline the administration and academic processes within an educational institution. In this system, the Head of Department (HOD) allocates subjects to teachers, who then update and manage the material related to their assigned subjects. Additionally, students have the capability to select and enroll in subjects of their interest. This system enhances organizational efficiency and facilitates communication between faculty and students.

The Travel Booking Microservices project is a sophisticated web application designed to streamline the travel booking and reservation process.
